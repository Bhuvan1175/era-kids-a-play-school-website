Freuently Asked Question

What are the admission criteria for your school?

At our school, admission for play school students is based on age eligibility, completion of required paperwork, including vaccination records, and occasionally a brief assessment to ensure a good fit for your child's developmental stage and our program.

What safety measures are in place within the school premises?

Rest assured, your child's safety is our top priority. We've implemented secure entry points, trained staff in first aid and emergency procedures, conduct regular safety drills, ensure thorough background checks for all employees, and adhere strictly to health and hygiene protocols.

What extracurricular activities does the school offer?

Our school offers a diverse range of extracurricular activities to enrich your child's educational experience, including sports, arts and crafts, music, drama, language clubs, and STEM programs. These activities provide opportunities for your child to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build friendships outside of the classroom.

What is the student-teacher ratio in classrooms?

Our classrooms maintain a low student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention and effective learning experiences. Typically, we strive to maintain a ratio that allows for ample interaction and support, fostering a conducive environment for academic and personal growth.

What support services are available for students with special needs?

We ensure all students have equal access to education, offering individualized plans, specialized instruction, learning accommodations, collaborative parent and specialist involvement, and a nurturing environment fostering inclusion and acceptance.
